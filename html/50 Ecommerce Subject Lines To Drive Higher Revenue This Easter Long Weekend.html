50 Ecommerce Subject Lines To Drive Higher Revenue This Easter Long Weekend

Anima Hossain
Mar 25, 2021
3 min read

Who says captivating holiday email campaigns are just for Christmas? Easter’s just around the corner, and with US total spending at $18 billion and the average individual spending of $151, it’s one of the most lucrative holidays after the Christmas season. As email marketers, you know how important subject lines are for getting noticed in a crowded inbox - over 35% of customers open emails based on subject lines alone! Use these 50 Easter subject lines to give Santa a run for his money and drive even higher sales during this Easter long weekend. Let’s hop in! 🐰

Wrap up

With Easter at your doorstep, there’s no better time to revamp your email campaigns with eggs-ceptional subject lines! Remember to engage customers by creating some FOMO with your subject lines, adding emojis to stand out in inboxes, and throwing in some Easter puns here and there, to sell more this Easter long weekend.
